= dynaru

Ruby Dynamo Clone

== What? Why?

Dynamo is lovely.  nosql was birthed by it and its influence is clear in the current best of breed.
I could do much worse than to go through the exercise of learning its bits by implementing them.
That may be all that ever comes of this, but maybe it will take off.
